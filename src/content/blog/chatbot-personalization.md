### Contextual Personalization
- **Time of Day**: Adjust tone and urgency
- **Device Type**: Optimize for mobile/desktop

- **Communication Style**: Formal vs. casual tone

- **Frequency**: Interaction preferences
- **Topics of Interest**: Relevant subject matter
