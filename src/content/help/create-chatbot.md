# How to create your first chatbot

Creating your first AI chatbot with CSMatic is quick and easy. Follow these simple steps to get started.

## Step 1: Sign up for an account

If you haven't already, sign up for a CSMatic account. You can start with our free tier to explore the platform.

## Step 2: Navigate to the dashboard

After logging in, you'll be taken to your dashboard. Click on the "Create New Chatbot" button in the top right corner.

## Step 3: Choose a template

Select a template that best fits your needs or start from scratch. We offer templates for customer support, lead generation, and more.

## Step 4: Configure your chatbot

Give your chatbot a name and customize its appearance to match your brand. You can change colors, add a logo, and customize the chat interface.

## Step 5: Train your chatbot

Upload your knowledge base or FAQs to train your chatbot. You can add documents, URLs, or manually input Q&A pairs.

## Step 6: Test your chatbot

Use the built-in testing interface to interact with your chatbot and ensure it's responding correctly.

## Step 7: Deploy your chatbot

Once you're satisfied with your chatbot's performance, click "Deploy" to make it live. You'll receive an embed code to add to your website.

## Next steps

After deploying your chatbot, you can continue to improve it by:

- Monitoring conversations and analytics
- Adding more training data
- Setting up integrations with your existing tools
- Customizing advanced settings
